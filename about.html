The Lobster Slipper Story


The Lobster Slipper was basically one of those playful ideas- just to see if it could be done and to have fun with everyday footwear. Lobsters, with their signature claws and red color, seemed like the perfect quirky, attention-grabbing design, and it tied in with the spirit of coastal life.
The goal was finding that delicate balance between bold aesthetics and practicality. We wanted these slippers to transition from lounging at home to walks along the beach.
Coming from the designers who love all things marine (and a great sense of humor) this concept has been fine-tuned to what it is today: an out-of-the-ordinary slipper.

RHS Student Store
Here at the Redmond High School Student Store, we take pride in being the heart of school spirit and community! Run by students, for students, our store offers a wide variety of RHS merchandise, snacks, and school essentials. We strive to create a welcoming space where students, staff, and families can find everything they need to show their Mustang pride. Whether you're grabbing a quick treat between classes or picking up some spirit gear for the next big game, the Student Store is here to serve you with enthusiasm and a commitment to making your high school experience unforgettable!
